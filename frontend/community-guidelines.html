Community Guidelines

EduGlow is built on trust. These rules protect students and teachers.

  • Be respectful and professional in all communication.
  • Do not share misleading qualifications or fake reviews.
  • Students must provide accurate contact details for trial requests.
  • Teachers should respond within 24 hours when possible.
  • No spam, harassment, or unsolicited messages.
  • Report issues immediately through the Contact page.

Accounts that violate these guidelines may be suspended.
